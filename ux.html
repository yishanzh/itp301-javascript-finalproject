User Experience Design Project


This is a self-initiated project.

It started with my own problem: one day when my friend and I were trying to dine in Little Tokyo in Los Angeles, it took us about half an hour to find a parking spot around the restaurant.

Therefore, I decided to find out whether people who dine or shop there have the same problem and tried to come up with a "solution".







Initial Problem Hypothesis:

People who dine in Little Tokyo, Los Angeles have a hard time finding a parking spot.

Process:

1. Interviewed 20 people who dine in Little Tokyo to validate my problem hypothesis.

2. Analyzed competitors

3. Storyboard building and prototyping

4. Made a pivot and rebuilt prototypes.

5. Built a landing page and ran Facebook Ad campaign.

Original Prototypes for a Parking Reservation App

Pivot:

After analyzing all the competitiors, I found out there are too many

competitiors in the market. I decided to make the parking reservation

function as a feature for Google Map, since Google Map already has

a large user base. See next slide for new prototyping screens.
